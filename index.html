This is the website for the Six Septembers: Mathematics for the Humanist reading group, by Patrick Juola and Stephen Ramsay. The book is available here as a PDF, but the full text will be on this site (with the authorsâ€™ approval) for reading group participants to annotate using Hypothesis.

To join the reading group, read our code of conduct and do any or all of the following:

  • Sign up for the Six Septembers Slack for asynchronous discussion
  • Fill out the Doodle poll for the June synchronous meeting
  • Join the mailing list for email updates (e.g. for new Doodle polls to plan synchronous meetings)
  • Join the Six Septembers Hypothesis group to annotate the text on this site (forthcoming)

Got questions? Email Quinn Dombrowski at qad@stanford.edu or tweet @quinnanya.
